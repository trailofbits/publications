# Buttercup and DARPA's AI Cyber Challenge

This presentation discusses Buttercup, Trail of Bits's automated, AI-powered system for finding and fixing bugs in open-source software, and its success in DARPA's AI Cyber Challenge where it won 2nd place and a $3 million prize.
