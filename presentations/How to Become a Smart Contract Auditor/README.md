Web3 security has seen billions lost to hacks, and auditors are the last line of defense. This talk breaks down what smart contract auditors do, how to get started in the field, and why it is one of the most impactful careers in web3 security. The presentation lays out a clear, actionable path for aspiring auditors, drawing from the speaker's own experience transitioning into a leading security role.
