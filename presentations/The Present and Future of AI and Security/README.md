Panel discussion held at Code Blue featuring insights from the AI Cyber Challenge (AIxCC) finals at DEF CON 33. The competition is a two-year national project led by DARPA and ARPA-H, aiming for the convergence of AI and cybersecurity with the goal of automated defense of software supporting critical infrastructure.

The panel features David Brumley and members of teams that placed highly in the AIxCC finals, exchanging views on the forefront and future of AI and cybersecurity.
