# Buttercup: The Future of Trail of Bits' Solution to DARPA's AI Cyber Challenge

This presentation explores the future direction of Buttercup, Trail of Bits' AI-powered automated system for finding and fixing bugs in open-source software, which won 2nd place and a $3 million prize at DARPA's AI Cyber Challenge.
