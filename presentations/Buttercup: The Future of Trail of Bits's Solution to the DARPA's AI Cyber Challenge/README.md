# Buttercup: The Future of Trail of Bits's Solution to the DARPA's AI Cyber Challenge

This presentation explores the future direction of Buttercup, Trail of Bits's AI-powered automated system for finding and fixing bugs in open-source software, which achieved 2nd place and a $3 million prize in DARPA's AI Cyber Challenge.
